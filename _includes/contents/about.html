About

Rikuto Nonomura was born in 2001. He is currently a doctoral student in the Function-Oriented Robotics Laboratory at the Graduate School of Engineering, Kobe University, Japan. His research theme includes lightweight environmental recognition and autonomous navigation for mobile robots． He likes watching football and Formula 1, and touring by motorcycle.

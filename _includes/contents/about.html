About

Rikuto Nonomura was born in 2001. He is currently a master's student in the Function-Oriented Robotics Laboratory at the Graduate School of Engineering, Kobe University, Japan. His research theme includes environmental recognition and autonomous navigation for long-term operation of mobile robots． He likes watching football and Formula 1 especially cheering for Shimizu S-Pulse and Yuki Tsunoda.
